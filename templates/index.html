This application allows you to export your recent Bookmarked Tweets.

To get started, we'll need you to authorize our demo App (Hello world twitter bot) to access your bookmarks on Twitter using OAuth 2.0. Learn more information about OAuth 2.0.

Here's how it works:

  1. We will redirect you to Twitter.com for you to authorize our app.
  2. While at Twitter.com, you will authorize our App to access your Bookmarks.
  3. After you click "Export CSV", you should get back a CSV of your most recent Bookmarks.
